Frequently asked questions

I’m new to benefits. How do I get set up?

Once you set up payroll with Gusto, you can view your recommended plans right from your account. Our fully-licensed advisors are also available to answer questions and help you choose a package that meets your budget and your team’s needs, should your group qualify for insurance plans. Have more questions? Call one of our licensed advisors today.

Are Gusto’s brokers licensed?

Absolutely! Gusto’s benefits advisors are fully-licensed professionals with years of experience. And they’re available by phone or email when you or your employees need some advice. Learn more

How does open enrollment work?

With Gusto, you get 100% online enrollment. Your employees can enroll in benefits, add dependents, and review their plan details in their Gusto account. We also have dedicated help just for employees to answer their benefits questions. Learn more about open enrollment here.

How do my employees make changes, like adding dependents?

Your employees can make updates right in their Gusto account. It’s easy to add a dependent, change an address, change a name, and more. See a demo.
